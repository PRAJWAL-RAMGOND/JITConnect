# 🚀 Deploy to Vercel - Quick Guide (5 Minutes!)

## 🎯 Fastest Method (Recommended)

### Step 1: Go to Vercel
Visit: **https://vercel.com**

### Step 2: Sign Up/Login
Click "Sign Up" → Choose "Continue with GitHub"

### Step 3: Import Project
1. Click "Add New..." → "Project"
2. Find your `JITConnect` repository
3. Click "Import"

### Step 4: Deploy
1. Leave all settings as default
2. Click "Deploy"
3. Wait 30-60 seconds ⏱️

### Step 5: Done! 🎉
Your site is live at: `https://jitconnect-[username].vercel.app`

---
